Relic Archive – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026‑03‑11

1. Who We Are

Relic Archive (“the App”) helps you document and preserve memories of personal items, with optional AI-assisted descriptions and classification.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data: your email when you register/sign in.
  • User content: titles, tags, descriptions, timeline notes, and media you upload (photos, audio, video, 3D, etc.).
  • Device permissions (only with your consent and when needed):
    • Camera – capturing item photos.
    • Microphone – recording voice notes you initiate.
    • Photo Library – selecting images from, or saving images to, your library.
    • Face ID/Biometrics – unlocking private content you choose to protect.
  • Purchases: subscription payments are processed by Apple. We do not collect your card details; we only receive status to activate entitlements.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide core features: item archives, collections, timelines, and media management.
  • AI generation: only when you request it, we send necessary input (e.g., title/context) to selected AI providers to generate text suggestions.
  • Security: account authentication and session management.

4. AI Providers and Sharing

When using AI features, we may send the minimal required inputs to third‑party AI providers to produce responses, such as:

  • Alibaba Cloud Tongyi (DashScope/Qwen)
  • Zhipu GLM
  • Google Gemini (used in eligible regions as fallback)

We never send your account password to any third party. You may use the App without AI features at any time.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Your content is stored on our servers or service environment solely to provide the App’s features to you.
  • We retain data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • You can edit or delete content you created (contact support if you need help beyond in‑app options).
  • You can disable camera/microphone/photo library/biometrics permissions at any time (some features may not work without them).

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact support.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact our support page: Support or email relicarchive@163.com.

This policy summarizes what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. We may update this policy; significant changes will be reflected on this page.
