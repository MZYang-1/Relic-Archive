Common Issues

  • Upload failures: Check network connectivity and camera/photo permissions; try again later.
  • Subscriptions not loading: Ensure the device can reach the App Store. In testing, sign in with a Sandbox tester account.
  • AI features unavailable: Could be network or provider limits. Non‑AI features remain usable.

When Contacting Support

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version/build (from Settings → About, or TestFlight/Test report)
  • Time of issue and steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if possible
